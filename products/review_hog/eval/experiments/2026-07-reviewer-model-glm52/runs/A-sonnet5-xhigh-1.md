- **Validator:** - **Checked:** the sole caller of `queue_inbox_pr_review`, what stamphog actually does with `acting_user_id`, and the webhook-leg belt-and-braces the finding cites as the missing precedent.

_perspective: review-hog-blind-spots-general · directly-related: True_

- **Problem:** `_format_self_driving(self, cl: dict)` renders its block purely off `cl.get("self_driving")`, with no visibility into `pr.author_is_bot` at all (its signature only receives `cl`, not `pr`).
